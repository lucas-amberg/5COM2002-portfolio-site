About This Project

Clear View is a window washing and solar cleaning business in Long Beach, CA. I was approached to help them with their website.

The Clearviews.pro website features all of the information about their business as well as a reviews section and a quoting form.

An administration panel is under development featuring client retention tools as well as ways to manage billing and invoices for customers.
