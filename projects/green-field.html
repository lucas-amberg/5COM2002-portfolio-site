About This Project

Green Field Churrascaria is a Brazilian Steakhouse in Long Beach, CA. My friend and I were approached to reconstruct their website.

The new Green Field Website features a new and improved reservation system with email confirmations and CRM features, as well as a robust announcement creator along with a new and improved design.

The site has been under development since May 2024 and will be releasing very soon.
